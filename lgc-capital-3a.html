Invoice Discounting Details

Use your accounts receivable to get immediate access to cash. You may sell some or all your invoices to LGC Capital, which then assumes the rights to collect the invoice payments.

How It Works

  • We setup a tripartite agreement with you, LGC capital, and your end customer.
  • LGC Capital secures credit insurance to protect against non-payment, and you agree to buy back the invoice if the client fails to pay.
  • You get an instant cash advance upon approval, and payment collection then transfers to LGC capital.

Benefits

  • Focus on your core business and customers and let us worry about payment collection.
  • Avoid missed payroll during low cash periods.
  • Reduce risk to your business through our credit insurance for non-payment.

Purchase Order Financing Details

Need to fulfil a large order but don’t have enough cash? We offer a tailor-made purchase order financing solution and would love to chat.

How It Works

  • You obtain instant capital for large orders upon approval.
  • We pay your suppliers directly, ensuring a smooth procurement process.
  • Finally, we handle customer payment collection and remit the balance to you.

Benefits

  • Fulfil much larger orders than you could on your own, and expand to new markets.
  • Better cashflow management—avoid tying up your cash in inventory or production costs.
  • We can often waive collateral for some businesses serving specific blue-chip clients.
